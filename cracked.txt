1. The Early Years Stay hungry, stay foolish! The story of Apple is a story of innovation. It began in a garage. We wanted to build a computer that was simple and beautiful. We had no idea it would change the world — forever. In the beginning, it was just Steve and me. We worked hard, we had fun, and we believed in our vision. 

The first Apple computer was a simple circuit board. It didn't have a case, a keyboard, or a screen. It was just a beginning, but it was our beginning. We showed it to our friends and they were amazed. We knew we were on to something. 

We faced many challenges in the beginning, but we never gave up. We believed in our vision, and we were determined to succeed. The story of Apple is a story of passion, hard work, and a commitment to excellence. 

The story continues... It was a cold morning in Cupertino. The sun was rising over the Silicon Valley. Inside the Apple headquarters, a team of engineers was working on a new project: the Macintosh. They had been working on it for months, and they were finally ready to show it to the world. 

The Macintosh was a revolutionary new computer. It had a graphical user interface, a mouse, and a simple, easy-to-use design. It was unlike anything anyone had ever seen before.
